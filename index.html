01

Design & Analysis of EPC Package

ISRO — Satellite Electrical Housing

Autodesk ANSYS GD&T FEA
▼

Objective

Design and analyze an electrical power conditioning package (EPC) housing for a satellite application — meeting structural, static load, and thermal requirements.

Engineering Approach

  • Built 3D model in Autodesk; applied GD&T to all drawings
  • Meshed the EPC package for FEA simulations in ANSYS
  • Performed modal, static, and thermal simulations
  • Evaluated stiffness, load resistance, and junction temperature

Results

Fundamental frequency of 793 Hz — met stiffness requirement
Withstood 15 g steady-state static load
Max junction temperature 107°C — within thermal limits

LU-HAP UAV Conceptual Design

NACDEC II — National Aerospace Competition

Li-S Battery Thin GaAs Solar LIDAR DoE
▼

Objective

Develop a LIDAR-equipped Unmanned High-Altitude Platform (LU-HAP) for coastal habitat mapping, shoreline change detection, and digital elevation modelling.

Engineering Approach

  • Literature survey on high power density batteries and solar cells
  • Design of Experiment (DoE) comparing energy storage technologies
  • Defined flight plan across 7-day coastal India mission profile
  • Assumed IRA conditions, no crosswinds, twilight LIDAR ops

Results

Selected Dual-junction Thin GaAs solar cells for high conversion efficiency
Selected Li-S battery: 435 Wh/kg specific energy, 1200 Wh/l density
Validated platform for long-endurance surveillance and mapping missions
